# RETURNRIDER PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated: June 4, 2026**

ReturnRider, Inc. ("**ReturnRider**," "**we**," "**us**") respects your privacy. This Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 1. Information We Collect

| Category | Examples | Source |
|----------|----------|--------|
| Account | Name, email, timezone | You |
| Connected email | Commerce-related headers/bodies (filtered), order/return metadata | OAuth (readonly) |
| Returns | QR data, deadlines, tracking numbers | Parsed + you |
| Financial (optional) | Transaction amounts, dates, merchant descriptors | Plaid (with consent) |
| Device | Push tokens, OS version, crash logs | Automatic |

**We do not** read your entire inbox for advertising profiles. Our systems use provider-side filters and classification to **ignore non-shopping email**.

## 2. How We Use Information

To provide return calendars, wallet passes, shipment updates, refund matching, customer support, security, and product improvement in aggregated/anonymized form. **We never sell your personal information to third-party advertisers.**

## 3. How We Share Information

We share with service providers under contract (hosting, email API access, EasyPost/Shippo, Plaid, push notifications, analytics in privacy-preserving mode) only to operate the Service. We may disclose if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

## 4. Email Parsing — Transparency

When you connect Gmail or Outlook, we request **read-only** mail permissions. We sync messages matching shopping-related criteria, extract order and return fields, and by default **do not retain full raw emails** beyond short encrypted TTL unless you enable extended retention. You can disconnect email access at any time, which stops future collection.

## 5. Data Retention

Account data: until deletion request + 30-day backup purge. OAuth tokens: deleted on disconnect. Parsed orders/returns: until you delete or account closure. Logs: 90 days (security: 1 year).

## 6. Security

AES-256 encryption for sensitive fields at rest; TLS 1.3 in transit; access controls and audit logging for employee access (prohibited except incident response).

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR / UK / CCPA)

- **Access / Portability:** Settings → Export My Data (JSON).
- **Deletion:** Settings → Delete Account.
- **Correction:** Edit return details or contact support.
- **Restrict / Object (GDPR):** privacy@returnrider.com.
- **CCPA:** We do not sell personal information. Limit use of sensitive financial data to refund features you enable.
- **Complaints:** You may contact your supervisory authority.

## 8. International Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States with appropriate safeguards (SCCs where applicable).

## 9. Children

Not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions).

## 10. Google & Apple Disclosures

Use of Gmail data complies with Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use requirements. Apple Privacy Manifest and App Store labels describe on-device data practices.

## 11. Changes

Material changes will be notified in-app or by email.
